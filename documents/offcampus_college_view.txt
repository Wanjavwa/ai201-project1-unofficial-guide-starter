Off-Campus Review: College View Flats
From the off-campus housing spreadsheet + reviews

College View is the opposite of Maple Court: more expensive, but you get what you pay for. Rent runs about $900/month for a shared two-bedroom, and most utilities (water, trash, internet) are included, so the real gap versus the cheap places is smaller than it looks.

The landlord (managed by Hawthorne Residential) is responsive — maintenance requests through their app usually get handled within 1-2 days. Several reviewers specifically called out that they got their full security deposit back, which is rare around here.

Amenities are the selling point: an actual gym in the building, in-unit laundry (not shared, not coin-op), reserved parking, and central AC that works. Units are newer and well-insulated, so heating bills in winter are low.

The main downside is distance. College View is about a 25-minute walk or a 7-minute bus ride from campus, on the north side. If you don't have a bike or a bus pass it can feel far, especially late at night.

Verdict: 4/5. The best-managed option near campus if your budget can handle it. Great for students who want reliability and amenities over being close to the quad.
