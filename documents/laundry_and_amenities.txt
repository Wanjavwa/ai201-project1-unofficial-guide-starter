Dorm Amenities Cheat Sheet (laundry, kitchens, AC) — community wiki

LAUNDRY
- Riverside Hall: FREE. No card, no quarters. Machines are newish, ~12 washers/10 dryers per building. Best laundry situation on campus.
- Westfield Suites: FREE, but fewer machines per resident, so weekends back up. Go on weekday mornings.
- North Tower: PAID. $1.50 per wash + $1.50 per dry, charged to your student card (no cash/coins). Basement only, often a wait Sunday nights.

AIR CONDITIONING
- Riverside: central AC, reliable.
- Westfield: central AC, reliable.
- North Tower: NONE. Bring a fan. This is the #1 complaint about North Tower.

KITCHENS
- Riverside: shared kitchen on every floor (this is why Riverside residents can opt into the smaller meal plan).
- Westfield: one shared kitchen per floor, suite common rooms have a microwave + mini-fridge.
- North Tower: a single basement kitchenette for the whole building; basically nobody uses it.

WIFI / OTHER
- All dorms have the same campus wifi (MSU-Secure). Wired ethernet jacks exist in every room and are faster/more stable for gaming.
- Quiet hours are 10pm-8am weekdays, midnight-9am weekends, enforced by RAs. Riverside is the least quiet in practice regardless of the posted hours.
