Dorm Review: Riverside Hall
From the freshman survival GroupMe

Riverside is the newest dorm (opened 2019) and it's where most first-years want to be. It has central AC, it's right next to the South Dining Hall (literally connected by a covered walkway, clutch in the rain), and it's the most social building on campus — if you want to meet people your first year, this is it.

The downside everyone warns you about: the walls are THIN. You will hear your neighbors' music, alarms, and arguments. Bring earplugs or a white-noise machine. It's also loud on weekends because it's the party dorm by reputation.

Rooms are standard doubles with hall bathrooms, but the bathrooms are modern and there are more of them per floor than the old dorms. Laundry is FREE in Riverside (no card, no quarters) which adds up over a year.

Because Riverside has a small shared kitchen on every floor, residents are allowed to choose the smaller "Block 80" meal plan instead of the required unlimited plan — one of the few dorms where that's an option.

Price-wise it sits in the middle: more than North Tower, less than Westfield.

Verdict: 4.5/5 for first-years who want the social experience and don't mind noise. Light sleepers, think twice.
