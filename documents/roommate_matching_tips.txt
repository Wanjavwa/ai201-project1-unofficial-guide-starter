Thread: How does roommate matching actually work at MSU?
Subreddit: r/MapleState

OP: Do I get to pick my roommate or does the school just throw me in with a stranger?

Answer (top): You have two paths.

1) Mutual request: If you already know who you want to live with, BOTH of you have to name each other in the housing portal, and you have to do it before the May 1 roommate-request deadline. One-sided requests don't work — if you list someone and they don't list you back, it won't match. Lock it in early; people forget and miss the deadline every year.

2) Matching questionnaire: If you don't have someone, you fill out a lifestyle questionnaire (sleep schedule, cleanliness, noise tolerance, study habits, guests). ResLife uses it to pair you. It's not magic but it's better than pure random — the cleanliness and sleep-schedule questions matter most, so answer those honestly, not aspirationally. Saying you're a neat early riser when you're not just gets you a mismatched roommate.

Reply: A few people forget that your roommate request and your room selection are separate. You can be matched with a roommate but still need a good time ticket to actually get a good room together. If your roommate has way more credits than you, use THEIR time ticket — the earlier of the two applies when you pull each other into a room.

Reply: If it doesn't work out, room-swap requests open about three weeks into the semester. Don't suffer in silence until then though — talk to your RA first, they can sometimes mediate.
